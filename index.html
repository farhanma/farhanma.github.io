I am Senior HPC System Engineer at Hewlett Packard Enterprise ( HPE ) responsible for the deployment, installation, configuration, supporting, and maintenance of HPC & AI solutions.

Prior to that, I worked as Senior Research Engineer at KAPSARC, Postdoctoral Researcher at KAUST with Professor David E. Keyes, Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville with Professor Jack Dongarra, as well as Software Engineer at the Saudi Electricity Company and Saudi Aramco.

From 2019 to 2021, I was part of the world's leading Exascale Computing Project of the United States Department of Energy: Software for Linear Algebra Targeting Exascale ( SLATE ).
